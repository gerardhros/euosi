# Example farm database to illustrate the functionality of the euosi package

Example farm database to illustrate the functionality of the euosi
package

## Usage

``` r
osi_farm
```

## Format

A data.frame with 60 rows and 24 columns:

- ID:

  the unique field-id

- B_COUNTRY:

  the country name

- B_LU:

  The BRP gewascode of the crop

- B_PREC_SUM:

  the total summer precipitation, ERA5 (mm)

- B_PREC_WIN:

  the total winter precipitation, ERA5 (mm)

- B_PET_SUM:

  the total summer evaporation, ERA5 (mm)

- B_PET_WIN:

  the total winter evaporation, ERA5 (mm)

- B_TEMP_SUM:

  the mean summer temperature, ERA5 (degrees C)

- B_TEMP_WIN:

  the mean winter temperature, ERA5 (degrees C)

- A_SOM_LOI:

  The percentage organic matter in the soil (%)

- A_CLAY_MI:

  The clay content of the soil (%)

- A_SAND_MI:

  The sand content of the soil (%)

- A_PH_CC:

  The acidity of the soil, measured in 0.01M CaCl2 (-)

- A_CACO3_IF:

  the percentage of CaCO3 (%)

- A_CEC_CO:

  The cation exchange capacity of the soil (mmol+ / kg), analyzed via
  Cobalt-hexamine extraction

- A_C_OF:

  The carbon content of the soil layer (g/ kg)

- A_N_RT:

  The organic nitrogen content of the soil in mg N / kg

- A_N_PMN:

  The potentially mineralizable N pool (mg N / kg soil)

- A_P_OL:

  The P-content of the soil extracted with Olsen (mg P / kg)

- A_K_AAA:

  The exchangeable K-content of the soil measured via ammonium acetate
  extraction (mg K / kg)

- A_B_HW:

  The plant available content of B in the soil extracted by hot water
  (mg B / kg)

- A_ZN_CC:

  The plant available content of Zn in the soil extracted by CaCl2 (mg
  Zn / kg)

- A_MG_AAA:

  The exchangeable Mg-content of the soil measured via ammonium acetate
  extraction (mg Mg / kg)
