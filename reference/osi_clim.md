# Averaged climatic conditions per country

Averaged climatic conditions per country

## Usage

``` r
osi_clim
```

## Format

A data.frame with 37 rows and 10 columns:

- osi_country:

  the country name

- b_prec_y:

  the total annual precipitation, ERA5 (mm)

- b_prec_sum:

  the total summer precipitation, ERA5 (mm)

- b_prec_win:

  the total winter precipitation, ERA5 (mm)

- b_pet_y:

  the total annual evaporation, ERA5 (mm)

- b_pet_sum:

  the total summer evaporation, ERA5 (mm)

- b_pet_win:

  the total winter evaporation, ERA5 (mm)

- b_temp_y:

  the mean annual temperature, ERA5 (degrees C)

- b_temp_sum:

  the mean summer temperature, ERA5 (degrees C)

- b_temp_win:

  the mean winter temperature, ERA5 (degrees C)
